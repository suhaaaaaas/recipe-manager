Spaghetti Bolognese|1 lb spaghetti, 1 lb ground beef, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 can crushed tomatoes, 1/4 cup tomato paste, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1/2 tsp dried basil, salt and pepper to taste|1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. 2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat. 3. Add chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened. 4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes. 5. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.|30|Main Course
